About Us

Who We Are

Athletix is Chesapeake, Virginia's leading soccer company dedicated to developing skilled players through professional training, quality equipment, and competitive events. Founded by passionate soccer enthusiasts, we strive to create an inclusive environment where players of all ages can grow and excel in the beautiful game.

Our mission is to foster a love for soccer in the community while providing the resources and opportunities needed for players to reach their full potential. Whether you're a beginner looking to learn the basics or an experienced player seeking to refine your skills, Athletix has something for everyone.

Our Services

Our Services

Training Programs

Professional coaching for all skill levels, from beginners to advanced players. Our structured programs focus on technique, tactics, fitness, and mental aspects of the game.

Tournaments & Events

Regular tournaments, friendly matches, and special events to test skills in a competitive environment. Perfect for team building and gaining real game experience.

Equipment & Gear

High-quality soccer equipment and apparel from top brands. We offer everything from soccer balls and cleats to training gear and team uniforms with professional fitting services.

Youth Programs

Specialized programs for children of all ages, designed to develop fundamental skills while keeping the game fun and engaging. We foster teamwork and build confidence in young players.
